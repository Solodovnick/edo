# Enterprise & communication

## When this skill applies

Use for tasks aligned with the **Enterprise & Communication** category in [anthropics/skills](https://github.com/anthropics/skills/tree/main/skills).

## Instructions

1. Match tone to audience (executive, engineering, end user) unless the user specifies otherwise.
2. Be concise and structured: headings, bullets, and clear calls to action where appropriate.
3. Avoid overclaiming; distinguish facts from recommendations.
4. For sensitive topics (legal, HR, security), suggest review by a qualified human; do not present generated text as legal advice.
